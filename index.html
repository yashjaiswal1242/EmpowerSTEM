Welcome to EmpowerSTEM

Inspiring the Innovators of Tomorrow

What is EmpowerSTEM?

Empower Stem was founded by high schoolers to educate the younger generation. Throughout the year, we offer FREE weekly engineering challenges for elementary and middle schoolers.

Are you 12 years old or younger? Are you creative? Do you like problem-solving? Do you enjoy engineering? Are you a kid? You are in the right place if you answered yes to any of these questions.

About

At EmpowerSTEM, we believe in empowering young minds to reach their full potential through hands-on learning and exploration. Our free STEM programs provide children aged 7-12 with the necessary skills to succeed in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. By engaging in interactive projects and experiments, we aim to spark a lifelong passion for learning and innovation. We are committed to educating the youth about the wonders of STEM, setting them up for future success. As we grow, we aspire to expand our organization to various locations in Connecticut and, possibly, nationwide. We envision a generation of well-equipped students to excel in their lives and contribute meaningfully to the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower the next generation of innovators, thinkers, and leaders in STEM fields. We strive to create a future where every student, regardless of background, has the tools, mentorship, and opportunities to explore their passions and achieve their dreams in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Together, we aim to spark curiosity, foster collaboration, and build a more inclusive and innovative tomorrow.

Our Values

Advocacy

Advocacy is a cornerstone of our Education Movement. Together, we amplify our voices and create impactful change. We strive to ensure that education is accessible and diverse for all. Interested in learning more? Join us and make a difference!

Partnership

At Empower STEM, we're dedicated to championing STEM education. By forming impactful partnerships with local schools and libraries, we aim to provide free, hands-on STEM education for kids in our community.

Outreach

EmpowerSTEM has built an environment of support and helpful resources to make sure that Outreach is always a priority. Our success is driven by the hearts, minds, and souls of our community. Come and join us.
